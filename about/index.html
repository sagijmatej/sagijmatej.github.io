F3 Architecture is a creative architectural firm with offices in Zagreb and Ivanec, dedicated to designing spaces that shape contemporary lifestyles. Through innovative design, precise execution, and sustainable concepts, our team develops architectural solutions that are functional, aesthetically appealing, and tailored to the needs of our clients. Regardless of the size or complexity of a project, our goal remains the same – to provide high-quality, long-lasting, and inspiring solutions that create comfortable environments.


Founder and Vision


The firm was founded by Filip Vusić, an architect with extensive experience in design and project management. Filip started his career with a vision of creating spaces that reflect modern needs while respecting the context and identity of each location. His work is characterized by attention to detail, innovation, and the ability to merge functionality with aesthetics.

Under his leadership, F3 Architecture has become synonymous with a contemporary approach to design, emphasizing execution quality, sustainability, and the smart use of resources. Filip believes that every space carries its own story, and his mission is to find the best way to express that story through architectural forms, materials, and design.


Our Locations


F3 Architecture operates through two strategically positioned offices to ensure close collaboration with clients and efficient project management:

Zagreb – Our main office in Zagreb focuses on larger architectural projects, urban regeneration, and commercial space design. Here, we develop conceptual solutions that redefine modern living and working environments, with an emphasis on cutting-edge aesthetics and practical functionality. Our collaborations span a broad range of clients, from private investors to corporate projects.

Ivanec – Our Ivanec office specializes in working with private clients, designing family homes and smaller residential buildings. Here, we focus on sustainable architecture, eco-friendly materials, and adapting spaces to their natural surroundings.

Our presence in two locations allows us flexibility and accessibility, while ensuring that each project is carefully crafted with attention to detail and long-term functionality.


Our Approach


At F3 Architecture, we believe that great design is not just about aesthetics – it must enhance how people experience and use space. Every project is developed through research, conceptualization, and precise execution, always respecting client wishes and the unique demands of each location.

Whether you're looking for a private residence design, business space, or a more complex architectural project, our team is here to shape spaces that align with your needs and aesthetic preferences.
